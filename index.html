How to use this recorder:

  1. Click "Start Recording" to begin
  2. Choose whether to record your entire screen, a specific application window, or a browser tab
  3. Grant microphone permissions when prompted
  4. Click "Stop Recording" when finished
  5. Use "Download Recording" to save your video

Note: For the best results, use Chrome, Edge, or Firefox. Safari has limited support for screen recording.
